# Energy Service Provision Analysis in Non-Interconnected Zones (ZNI) of Colombia

## 🌎 Language

> 📖 [Read in English](README.md) | [Leer en Español](README_ES.md)

This project delivers a comprehensive analysis and an interactive dashboard focusing on the status of electricity service provision in Colombia's Non-Interconnected Zones (ZNI / NIA). The primary objective is to evaluate coverage, service availability (average hours of supply per day), geographical distribution, and energy infrastructure performance across remote Colombian regions.

---

## 📊 Data Sources

The data driving this analysis is sourced from official and public data repositories of the Republic of Colombia:

1. **Original Source:** [Colombia Open Data Portal - Ministry of Mines and Energy](https://www.datos.gov.co/Minas-y-Energ-a/Estado-de-la-prestaci-n-del-servicio-de-energ-a-en/3ebi-d83g/data_preview)
2. **Kaggle Dataset:** [Energía en Zonas No Interconectadas de Colombia](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andreafs/energa-en-zonas-no-interconectadas-de-colombia)

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- **Continuity Monitoring:** Analyze the average hours of daily energy service received by communities living within the ZNI.
- **Territorial Distribution:** Identify and map the departments and municipalities experiencing high energy vulnerability and reliance on localized solutions (e.g., diesel generators or hybrid systems).
- **Capacity Analysis:** Assess the relationship between installed generation capacity, overall demand, and the total number of affected departments, municipalities and localities.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Deliver an interactive visual tool tailored for public policy development, renewable energy investment, and rural energy grid optimization.

## 📈 Dashboard Architecture

The interactive dashboard is organized into the following analytical sections:

- **Overview Canvas:** Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) showcasing total users, average daily service hours, and total installed capacity.
- **Geographical Insights:** Dynamic map visualizations identifying critical zones with critical service discontinuity.
- **Efficiency & Infrastructure:** Granular breakdowns of generation technologies and their impact on energy supply reliability.
